Anureet Dhaliwal

Fitness Trainer and Operator of A1 Fitness

Anureet Dhaliwal is a personal trainer based in the Los Angeles area with a masters in fitness medicine and over 10 years in personal training experience. Anureet has owned her own gym (at the age of 23) and has competed in various triathlons, but has found her calling in one on one personal training. Eventually, Anureet wants to open a gym that serves as a rehabilitation center and as a place for children with Austism to be able to workout and maintain healthy lives.

Anureet's goal orientated lifestyle bleeds into her client workouts, as she strives for the completion of their own goals. Overall, Anureet wants her clients to achieve physical, mental, and spiritual health so that they may live their best lives - free from doubt and self consciousness. Whether you want to meet in person or over Zoom, Anureet will put in the work to make sure you get results.

"Seeing strength build in others is the most emotional part for me"
- Anureet Dhaliwal
